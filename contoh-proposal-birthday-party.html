1. contoh invitition birthday party???


Who sends the invitation? (Siapa yang mengirim surat?)Who is invited to the birthday party? (Siapa yang diundang ke pesta ulang tahun?)When will be the birthday party? (Kapan acara pesta ulang tahun berlangsung?)Where will be the birthday party? (Dimana acara pesta ulang tahun berlangsung?)
Ingat, Who, who, when, where, pokoknya. Artinya, siapa yang ngundang, siapa yang diundang, kapan waktu acara berlangsung, dan di mana tempat acara berlangsung. Itu saja hal penting dalam birthday invitation.
Contoh Undangan Ulang Tahun Unik

9. She _____ to my birthday party​


Jawab.

She comes to my birthday party

Kenapa menggunakan comes dan bukan come ?

Karena subject-nya adalah She (dia)

Jika subject berupa He/She/It, maka verb(kata kerja) yang digunakan ditambah dengan s/es/ies

Tapi, jika subject yang digunakan merupakan I/you/they/we, maka tidak perlu menambahkan s/es/ies

Penggunaan es/ies

ada beberapa verb yang menggunakan es dan bukan s

contohnya : watch → watches

Ada pula yang menggunakan ies

contohnya : study → studies

mengapa demikian? karena bunyi huruf terakhir dari verb study /studi/ adalah i

16. buat kalimat asking and giving offer dengan contoh kalimat attending birthday party


Party Invitations
The whole class sits in a circle! Tell them that it’s your birthday next week and that you’re planning a birthday party. They are all invited... but on one condition. They must bring you a present, and it must be something that you really want. Each student in turn tells you what they will bring to give you on your birthday. You will either tell them that they can come, or that they are not invited. This depends on what they offer to bring you. The item they’re going to bring must begin with the same letter as your first name. If it does, they can come; if it doesn’t, they can’t. For example, if your name is Lucy and they offer to bring ‘a lemon’ as a present, they will be welcome. If they offer to bring ‘a bottle of wine’ they will be given short shrift! This game is hilarious, as some students will twig onto your ‘unspoken rule’ fairly early on, while some won’t get it at all, however obvious you make it!
